About the Project

SmartWasteAI is a comprehensive system built on the pillars of Deep Learning and the Internet of Things (IoT), aiming to modernize traditional waste management cycles. Our core goal is to shift the paradigm from reactive waste collection to a proactive, data-driven approach.

The system's intelligence stems from two key components: a high-accuracy image classification model that identifies waste materials at the source (differentiating between Biodegradable, Non-Biodegradable, etc.), and IoT sensors utilized for real-time monitoring of collection bins across a geographic area.

This project is developed by Rishit Mohanty for academic and developmental purposes, showcasing a viable and scalable solution for smart city infrastructure to promote environmental sustainability and enhance resource recovery accuracy.

Core Objectives & Mission

Sustainability & Resource Purity

To promote highly effective recycling by minimizing contamination. By using AI classification, we ensure that waste is properly categorized for optimal reuse and resource purification.

Operational Efficiency & Automation

To drastically reduce collection costs and labor hours, the combination of AI predictions and IoT monitoring enables dynamic route optimization, ensuring vehicles only travel when bins are nearing capacity.
